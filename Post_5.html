Discounts

In this lesson we went over everything we have learnt and tidied up our blog. This half term we have learnt 2 major things. VLOOKUPS and IF statements. VLOOKUPS basically look up a column (v for vertical) and do something using that. EG: =VLOOKUP(B2, 'Business Model'!A:B, 2, false) looks at the B2 finds that in the "Business Model" tab in the spreadsheet where the items and values are found (A = items B = prices) and sees what item you have chosen and puts in the price. Then you can do things like add up all the prices for your total. We also looked at if statements. A conditional statement which does an action IF something is true/false.
