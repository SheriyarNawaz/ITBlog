Writing Our Names

In this lesson we returned to turtle and started learning how we could go about writing our names in turtle. My name is really difficult as it has a curved letter straight away, an S. I managed it by using a for loop to basically say turtle go forwards once, turtle go right at a 1 degree angle. I did that with a range of 270 degrees so I get a 270 degree curve. Then I repeated that but making the turtle go left instead of right.
