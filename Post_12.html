Buzzfeed Quiz

In this lesson we started of by doing a bunch of buzzfeed quizzes. We realised that they are very inaccurate but must have different mathmatical ways to gather all the data. For example: each answer you press adds a certain number onto the value for a certain colour(for example) which onto a sort of tally in the background. At the end the colour with the highest value was the colour you got. Then we started planning our own buzzfeed quizzes thinking of the maths behind it and the questions and options needed.
